For leading the IT Club hackathon, I organized a team of 15 members with different skill levels. I started by breaking down the project into manageable tasks and assigning them based on each member's strengths. I held weekly check-in meetings to track progress and address blockers. When conflicts arose between team members about technical approaches, I facilitated discussions to find consensus. We successfully delivered the event with 50+ participants, and all team members reported positive feedback about the collaboration experience.
